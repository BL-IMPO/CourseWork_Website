Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your data

Introduction

At ReadScore, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our text analysis services.

Information We Collect

Text Content

When you use ReadScore to analyze text, we process the content you provide solely for the purpose of generating readability scores and linguistic analysis. Your text is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently on our servers.

Usage Information

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our services, including:

  • Analysis request frequency
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Performance metrics
  • Error reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide text readability analysis services
  • Improve and optimize our algorithms
  • Enhance user experience and service performance
  • Ensure the security and reliability of our platform
  • Develop new features and functionality

Data Protection & Security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

Text content submitted for analysis is processed in memory and is not persistently stored. Anonymous usage data may be retained for service improvement purposes.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your text content with third parties. Our service may use trusted third-party services for infrastructure and analytics, but these services only receive anonymous, aggregated data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about how your data is processed
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Ask questions about our privacy practices

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or data practices, please contact us.
