Common Issues & Solutions

Text Analysis Not Working

Ensure your text meets the minimum requirements (at least 50 words recommended for accurate analysis). Check that the text is in a supported language (currently optimized for English).

File Upload Issues

We support TXT, DOC, DOCX, and PDF files. Make sure your file is not password protected and is under 10MB in size.

Understanding Readability Scores

Different readability formulas measure different aspects of text complexity. Check our documentation for detailed explanations of each score and what they mean.
